About Us

Who We Are

We are Aditya Rauthan (Class 12, Biology) and Shreshtha Oberoi (Class 12, Maths) – two passionate students who came together with a single goal: to build a platform that supports rookie entrepreneurs in every step of their journey.

Our Story

The idea for StartWise came from Aditya's father, who inspired us to think about how young minds can shape the future with guidance and resources. Together, we expanded the idea and began working on this site, starting with HTML as a static site. While this is just the beginning, we are committed to making it bigger and better with time.

Our Mission

To provide all-in-one support for young entrepreneurs: guidance, resources, opportunities, and success stories that help them learn, grow, and succeed.

Our Vision

We dream of building a platform that connects aspiring entrepreneurs with the right tools, mentors, and opportunities – from India to the world. StartWise will grow into a hub where ideas take shape and future leaders are born.
